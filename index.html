How to study effectively with ChemLab

1) Start with functional groups

Recognize the key reactive site first, then predict likely electrophile/nucleophile behavior.

2) Focus on conditions + mechanism

For each reaction, memorize conditions and understand why the mechanism gives that product.

3) Compare competing outcomes

Use the RuleBook to distinguish Markovnikov vs anti-Markovnikov, Zaitsev vs Hofmann, and related patterns.

4) Revise with checklists

Use the built-in revision points and mistake lists to prepare for school and university-level exams.
